Tell Us About Your Operation

These questions help us identify potential issues faster. Answer what you can — skip anything you're unsure about.

What type of crop insurance do you currently have?

Select all that apply. If unsure, that's OK — we'll identify it from your documents.

What coverage level do you carry?

This is a percentage, typically between 50% and 85%.

What unit structure does your policy use?

This affects how losses are calculated. Enterprise units combine all fields; optional/basic units keep them separate.

Do you use irrigated or non-irrigated practices?

Mismatched practice codes are one of the most common errors we find.

Do any of your crops follow another crop in the same season?

For example: double-crop soybeans after winter wheat. This affects your practice code and premium.

Do you currently carry any supplemental coverage?

Select all that apply.

Have you ever had a prevented planting claim?

This helps us check whether your prevented planting provisions are set up correctly.

Is there anything specific you're concerned about with your current policy?

This helps us focus our analysis. Mention anything — premium cost, coverage gaps, yield history, or anything your agent told you that didn't sound right.

Do you know your approximate APH (Actual Production History) yields?

If you uploaded your APH records, you can skip this. Otherwise, give us your best estimates.

Anything else we should know?

Changes in acreage, new crops, changes in farming practices, recent land purchases — anything that might affect your coverage.
